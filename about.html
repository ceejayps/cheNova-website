About Che' Nova

Che' Nova Lane is the owner and founder of Che' Nova Aesthetics in Towson, Maryland.


Che' Nova Lane
Proprietor- Certified Esthetician (Owner and Founder)


Che' Nova has always been passionate about the nature and health of skincare; and it was this passion that brought Che' to the realization of how skin care could be life changing for those who embraced its practices .
Che' decided early on in her journey to expand her knowledge in skin care. She went forward to study at the renowned Von Lee International School for Esthetics.
Che’ chose to specialized in skin care because it is her philosophy that by improving a woman’s skin it would enhance her self-image and confidence as well as improve her overall health.
Che' Nova is determined to be on the forefront of the beauty industry and to evolve in the field of aesthetics as that field presents new products, technology and methods to improve skin health. Che' has committed herself to aesthetics and its principles and is determined to bring forth her skills and knowledge to lead the skincare industry.
