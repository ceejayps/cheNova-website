Our Services

Che' Nova is especially known for her facials and extractions, stimulating the facial tissues and removing blackheads, whites heads and oil deposits, leaving the skin feeling and looking radiant. The products used in the spa are the absolute best in quality and can be purchased at the spot for your personal regimen.

Che' Nova is a perfectionist, especially when it comes to waxing. Whether a bikini wax or a Brazilian wax, Che' Nova will make the waxing experience a pleasant one; and her special techniques and products will make the experience nearly painless. Che' Nova's care and attention to detail will always give the client a satisfying hairless result.

Facials

A series of treatments performed for the purposes of beautifying your skin, in some cases treat a specific condition.

Dermaplaning

Dermaplaning is a mechanical form of exfoliation that gently removes dead skin cells and peach fuzz from the epidermis..

Brow Tinting

Brow tinting enhances the color of the brow by using semi-permanent dye to tint the brows.

Lash Extensions

Lash Extensions enhances the length, structure, and fullness of the natural lash.


Waxing

Waxing removes unwanted hair from different parts of the body. (Hard and soft wax are available)

Chemical Peels

Chemical Peels use specific chemicals to remove dead skin cells, treat acne and hyperpigmentation as well as other skincare conditions.

Brow Shaping

Enhances the brows by removing unwanted hair to give a desired look, according to the structure of your face.
